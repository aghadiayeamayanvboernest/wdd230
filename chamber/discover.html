Demographics

Population: 2,000,000; Median Age: 30; Age Distribution: 30% Children, 40% Adults, 30% Seniors.

Benin City, the vibrant heart of Edo State, is rich in culture, history, and economic opportunities. From its iconic bronze sculptures that highlight its deep-rooted artistry to its bustling marketplaces and thriving industries, Benin offers an unforgettable experience for visitors and investors alike. Discover the city’s cultural heritage, explore emerging business opportunities, and enjoy the warmth of its people. Whether you’re here to experience the historical landmarks or to explore the growing commerce and trade sector, Benin City is a destination filled with promise and potential.
